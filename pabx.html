PABX Systems

A Private Automatic Branch Exchange (PABX) is a private telephone network that allows a business to connect internally and to the public phone network. PABX systems are essential for large businesses and call centers that need to handle a high volume of calls.

Video Conference System

A video conference system is a hardware and software setup that allows people in different locations to communicate in real-time via a network. It uses devices with cameras and microphones to simulate a face-to-face meeting.

PA Conference Systems

A PA system may include multiple microphones or other sound sources, a mixing console to combine and modify multiple sources, and multiple amplifiers and loudspeakers for louder volume or wider distribution. Horn loudspeakers are often used to broadcast sound in outdoor locations.
